Technology Leadership

Technology Leadership Team

Ed Shea

Chief Technology Officer

Ed Shea is the Chief Technology Officer at Geode. Mr. Shea is responsible for the overall management of the firm’s Technology infrastructure, including the planning and execution of Geode’s strategic Technology initiatives. Mr. Shea joined Geode in 2002. He holds a B.A. in Economics and an M.S. in Computer Science, both from Boston College.

Doug Eisenstein

Head of Cloud and Data

Doug Eisenstein is the Head of Cloud and Data at Geode. His team is responsible for trusted and actionable data in a modern cloud-native platform backed by a team of world-class engineers. Mr. Eisenstein joined Geode in 2021. He was Founder of Advanti, a FinTech company, for 20 years, where his team serviced the needs of over 41 investment management firms across the globe.

Yousef Hamade

Head of Technology Services

Yousef Hamade is Head of Technology Services at Geode. Mr. Hamade is responsible for overseeing Technology Operations, including Platform & Security Engineering, IT Governance, and Program Management. Mr. Hamade join Geode in 2021. He holds multiple technology and cybersecurity related professional certifications including Certified Information Systems Security Professional and Certified Cloud Security Professional from (ISC)2, Certified Information Security Manager from ISACA, Project Management Professional from PMI, as well as multiple other technology specific certifications.

Zac Sheffer

Head of Architecture and Engineering

Zac Sheffer is Head of Architecture and Engineering. Mr. Sheffer is working with the Technology team to build the firm’s next generation cloud solutions for portfolio management, operations, trading and business development. He holds a BS in Mechanical Engineering from Northeastern University.
